# Social Media Checklist
## Do’s
- Think before you post, consider how it may affect your reputation
- Keep your personal information (like address, phone number, passwords) private
- Use privacy settings to control who can see your content
- Be respectful and positive in your interactions with others
- Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation
## Don’ts
- Don’t share sensitive or confidential information
- Don’t engage in cyberbullying, trolling, or harmful comments
- Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
- Don’t overshare your daily life or location publicly
- Don’t believe or forward unverified news or rumors
