## Stay Safe Online
- Use strong, unique passwords and enable 2FA (2 factor authentication) on important accounts 
- Avoid public Wi-Fi for banking or logins to sensitive accounts 
- Verify links, emails, and messages before clicking any links 
- Download apps only from trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store 
- Limit personal information shared on social media 
- Never share UPI PIN or OTP with anyone 
- Check UPI ID and name carefully before sending money 
- Report cyber fraud immediately via National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930
